Global Landscape of Climate Finance

2021

Total climate finance has steadily increased over the last decade, reaching USD 632 billion in 2019/2020.

But will that be enough to keep Earth from warming more than 1.5 degrees Celsius?

And what will we need in 2050?

Climate finance must increase by at least 588% to USD 4.3 trillion by 2030 to meet our climate objectives.
